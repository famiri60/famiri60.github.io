About Me

I find joy in the art of data exploration, like a detective uncovering clues. My expertise lies in sifting through vast datasets, decoding their stories, and translating them into actionable insights. I believe that data holds the key to solving some of the world's most challenging puzzles, and I'm here to be part of that solution. My graduate study at Georgia Tech in data analytics not only honed my technical abilities in programming, machine learning, NLP, and database management but has also sharpened my soft skills. I thrive on solving complex problems, excel in effective communication, and cherish the dynamics of teamwork. My journey began in the world of engineering, where I have been engaged in laboratory research for several years. This hands-on experience was invaluable. It allowed me to generate data through meticulous design of experiments, conduct factor screening and optimization, delve into exploratory data analysis, and harness the power of statistical and mathematical programming. Furthermore, I became proficient in crafting comprehensive reports and visually engaging representations. I am excited to be part of the data revolution and eager to collaborate on projects that involve the magic of numbers and the art of storytelling through data. Whether you seek insights, solutions, or innovative data-driven strategies, I am here to help. Feel free to reach out and let's embark on a data-driven journey together.
